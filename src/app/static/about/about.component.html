ABOUT PolTrack.org

PolTrack.org is a government accountability project of Voter Information Services, a US-based, 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.

PolTrack.org is the latest version of a non-partisan voter education tool that had started as an email newsletter called VIS Ratings of the U.S. Congress in 1995, became the website www.vis.org in 1997, and was most recently known as VIS Congressional Report Cards.
